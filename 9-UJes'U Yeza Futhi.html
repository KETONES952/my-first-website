9 UJes’ U Yeza Futhi!

Khalisan’ icilongo kakhulu;

uJes’ u yeza futhi;

Bonke ba bhekisise phezulu

uJes’ u yeza futhi

Chorus:

’Yeza futhi! Yeza futhi!

uJes’ u yeza futhi!


Memezani naseziqongweni,

uJes’ u yeza futhi;

Li za nobukhosi iZinyane,

uJes’ u yeza futhi;


Hlokomanini lwandle namaza,

uJes’ u yeza futhi;

Qubukani kanjalo zinhlanzi,

uJes’ u yeza futhi;


Zi yasitshela iziboniso,

uJes’ u yeza futhi;

Nawo umoya u yavunguza,

uJes’ u yeza futhi;


Izizwe zonke zi thukuthele,

uJes’ u yeza futhi;

Nalo ulwazi futhi lwandile,

uJes’ u yeza futhi;
