1 Vuthela Icingo

Mlindi, vuthel' icilongo, Xwayis' abadukile;

Lowoozway' icilongo, Angaphenduk' aphile.

Chorus:

Vuthel' icilongo, mlindi

iNkos' ithi; Vuthela...

Li vuthelefuthi-futhi;

Bonke bangasindiswa


Li khalis' eziqongweni, Endaweningendawo;

Ba solwandle memezani: Indaba yokuphila.


Yimemeze endleleni, Yith' "Into zilungile;"

Ku semnyama emhlabeni, uBab' u silindile.


Bathsele abasindiswayo, uJes' u yabanxusa.

U yasho kwabamdingayo: "Wozani niphiliswe".
