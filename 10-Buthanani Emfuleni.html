10 Buthanani Emfuleni

Sobuthana emfulweni,

Kanye nezingilosi

Amanz’ ephum’ es’Hlalweni;

KuJehova, iNkosi

Chorus:

Yebo! sobuthana khona,

Emfulweni omuhle khona le

Nabangcwele sohlangana;

Emfulwen’ ocwebile


Khona ogwini lomfula,

Sohamba si khuleka

Si dumisa ngokuthula,

Imihla yethu yonke


Emfuleni wokukhanya,

Si zolahla umthwalo

Ngomusa si zokhululwa,

Samkele imiqhele


Emfuleni ocwebile,

Alusekho usizi;

Inhliziyo zi thabile,

Ngehubo elimnandi
