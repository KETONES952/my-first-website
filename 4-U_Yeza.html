4 U Yeza

Imnand’ indab’ endlebeniyesihambi,

Esazulakudenekhaya!

Ku seduzeabuyengodum’ uMhlengi;

Masiny’ uMbus’ u fike.

Chorus:

U yeza, u yeza, masinyane,

U yez’ emhlabenilapha;

Izihambizonazifumanise IkhayaloMsindisi.


Bonke labo abakade ba ngcwatshwayo,

Ba zovuka ekupheleni.

Ye nabasolwandle olujulileyo;

Ukuphila bokufumana.


Kwelisha ilizwe si zohlanganiswa;

Si hub’ ingoma yobukhosi

Nabavela eNyakatho ba zokuza,

Ba khonz’ iNkosi yamakhosi.


Haleluya, amen! Haleluya futhi;

Ngokwethembeka sobakhona.

Lindani, thembani, kube isikhathi;

Umqhele sesowufumana.
