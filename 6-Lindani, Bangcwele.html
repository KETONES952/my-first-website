6 Lindani, Bangcwele

Lindani, khaliphanini;

Bonani iziboniso,

Kanyisani izibani;

Ngob’ uJes’ u lusindiso

Chorus:

U yeza! Jes’ u yeza! U yeza,

UJesu u zakubusa,

U yeza ngosindiso!


Isithembiso seNkosi;

Umusa owathengwayo,

Zon’ izembatho zegazi;

Zi sitshel’ indaba yazo.


Im’buso I yachitheka;

Yizw’ amasond’ egedleza.

Ngomsa owandile landa;

Icilongo li mpempeza!


Zoni, uKrest’ u yanxusa;

KuYise u sanimele

Phangisan’ u khon’ umusa;

Angakathi; Ku phelile
